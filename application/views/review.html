Crystal Ballroom

The Grand Printzard Hotel - Bangkok

Crystal Ballroom, recognised as one of the largest hotel’s ballrooms in the city, features a total space of 900 square metres (9,687 square feet). The room can host up to 1,200 guests for a cocktail reception or 950 guests for theater-style meeting. The specious and welcoming ballroom foyer, including the balconies, is ideal to host pre-event reception, product showcase or a themed coffee break.
